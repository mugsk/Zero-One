SchoolManager

Product description

SchoolManager is a flagship educational and school management solution from Zero&One Technologies. SchoolManager is a distributed web-based software solution that enables school administrators, parents and teachers store, access, view and update relevant student data and records from anywhere and at anytime using any device of their choice, computers, tablets or mobile phones. The goal of the system is to improve information management within the school by developing a system that ensures data is readily available, up-to-date, searchable and fully integrated with automated generation of reports.

SchoolManager has graphic dashboards that teachers, administrators, students, parents can use to simplify tasks and view all the information they require for participation in school activities. SchoolManager modules manage all of a school’s processes from conduct of admission and eligibility tests, admission of new students, preparation of results, testimonials, and transfer certificates amongst others. School Manager has modules for managing Timetable, Attendance, Examinations, Gradebooks and School Calendar.

SchoolManager emphasizes Simplicity, Efficiency, Ease of Use and Affordability and is the easiest and most efficient school management system to get your school up and running in a few days.

E-Slate

Product description

The E-Slate is an ultra-portable learning device that offers a complete learning solution. The device is pre-installed with a Library of curriculum books which students can access both offline and online. The E-Slate provides each student with a personal library of books and other learning resources as well as a record of their assignments and record of achievements. This will enable students to have access to their books and learning resources anywhere and at anytime. The development of the E-Slate is in line with the goals of the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) objectives for the digitalization of learning resources and adoption of technology in the classroom to increase access to quality education and deepen the learning process. The provision of E-Slates to students will help address the problems associated with shortage of learning materials while enhancing the learning experience by providing multimedia and other intelligent learning tools. The use of technologies such as the E-Slate has been shown to correlate with a marked improvement in the performance outcomes of students. The E-Slate Tablet and software solutions are available in several configurations for institutions at various levels of education such as:

  1. Primary Schools
  2. Secondary Schools
  3. Tertiary Institutions
  4. Training Institutes

There are also exam preparation modules targeted at self-learners preparing for major examinations such as Common Entrance Examinations, Junior School Certificate Examinations, Senior School Certificate Examinations, and University Matriculation Examinations and other Examinations in Tertiary Institutions. The E-Slate platform offers schools several deployment options

  1. Turnkey: This solution provides the E-Slate Hardware, Software and alongside learning content to schools as a single offering.
  2. Software: This solution licenses the E-Slate learning software to schools to adapt in creating content.
  3. Content: This solution provides schools with digital textbooks and content for distribution to their students in both offline and online environments.
  4. Custom: This solution provides schools tools, technologies, training, software and skills to digitalize their lesson notes and digitalize their content.
